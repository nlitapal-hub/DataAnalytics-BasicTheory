## Data Analytics
- Importance of Data Analytics:-
1)Decision Making:- clear facts and patterns from data helps in informed decision making

2)Problem Solving :- points out the problem and why making it easier to fix.

3)Identify opportunities :- shows trends and new chances for growth that might not be obvious.

4)Improved Efficiency:- reduces waste, saves time and makes work smoother by finding better ways to do things.


## Process of Data Analytics:-

1)Data Collection :-Raw data from websites, apps, survey

2)Data Preparation:- Cleaning data by fixing errors, handling missing values and removing duplicates 

3)Data Analysis:-Python, R,SQL or Excel to find pattern and extract meaningful insights

4)Data Visualization:-data through charts and graphs.

## Types of Analytics:-

1)Descriptive : Summarize and understand past data.

2)Diagnostic: tools like correlation, regression or comparisons to find the cause of a problem.

3)Predictive: current and past data to find patterns and make forecasts.

4)Prescriptive: Helps choose the best action or solution.


## Method of Data Analytics:-

1)Qualitative Data Analytics:-

doesn’t uses statistics and derive data from the words ,pictures and symbols.

2)Quantitative Data Analytics:-

collect data and then process it into the numerical data.

## Types of Data

1)Quantitative Data :- it can be counted and expressed in numerical form. can be used for statistical analysis.

2)Qualitative Data :- non- numeric information that explains qualities

3)Structured Data :-information organized into a prdefined format (rows and columns)

4)Unstructured Data :- raw data that doesn’t follows any predefined structures.

5)Semi-Structured Data:-combination of aspects of structured and unstructured data, not tables are involved but it can contain tags or marker.

## BIG DATA

Big data refers to the data that is too large,too varied and too fast to be handled by traditional data processing tools.

[]Volume: Massive amounts of data
[]Velocity: speed of generation and processing
[]Variety: Different format: structured,unstructured and semistructured
[]Veracity: Accuracy andtrustworthyness of the data to deal with noise and errors.
[]Value: usefulness of data
